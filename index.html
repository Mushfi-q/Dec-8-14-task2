FAQ

Got Questions?

We've Got You Covered.

From case tracking to certified copies - find answers to everything you need to know.

01

How do I track my case?

Need to do a one-time setup for linking your cases. You'll be notified about hearing dates, next posting, progress, and court records.

02

What kind of updates will I get?

You'll be notified about hearing dates, next posting, progress, and court records.

03

How do I request a CTC?

You can request a Certified True Copy (CTC) directly through the app. Simply navigate to the document section and follow the prompts to submit your request.

04

How do alerts work?

Alerts are automatically sent to your registered mobile number and email whenever there's an update on your case, such as hearing dates, orders, or judgments.
